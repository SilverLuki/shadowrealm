📜 Lore of the Archive

Deep within Duel Academy lies a system that predates the modern era — the Shadow Realm Archive. Built by Maximillion Pegasus during the founding of Industrial Illusions, it holds every duel ever recorded, every card ever created, and secrets that were never meant to surface.

Pegasus encrypted the deepest vault with a four-digit Millennium code, known only to him. Legends say those who breach the Archive will find knowledge powerful enough to challenge the Shadow Realm itself.

"The Millennium Eye sees all who seek the forbidden knowledge. Are you worthy, duelist?"
